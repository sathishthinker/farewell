Chapter by Chapter

The Journey 🗺️

From July 27, 2021 to March 11, 2026 — nearly 5 years of Access & Provisioning work, escalations handled, friendships made, and Power Automate flows that changed how the team operates. Here's how it all went down.

July 27, 2021 — Day One

🐣Entered HCLTech

Walked through the doors on July 27th as a fresh Senior Analyst, slightly nervous, fully committed. The world of Access & Provisioning opened up fast — onboarding flows, role assignments, entitlement reviews, access certifications. Complex, meticulous, and completely absorbing. This is where the story began.

2022 — Making Friends & Handling Escalations

🤝Friendships Forged Under Fire

Nothing bonds a team faster than escalations. While tickets flew and pressure mounted, I found my people. Through the chaos of urgent requests and tricky access issues, real friendships were built — the kind that survive even the most painful audit season.

2023 — Getting Hands-On With Peers

⚙️Learning by Doing, Together

This was the year of getting truly hands-on — understanding how employee onboarding and termination flows really work, tracing access paths, managing role conflicts, and learning from peers who knew the system inside out. Hands-on experience earned through real provisioning challenges, not manuals. Every access request resolved together made the knowledge stick.

2024 — Discovering Power Automate

🔬The Research Phase Begins

Started asking: "What if we could automate this?" Dove deep into Power Automate research — exploring how workflows could eliminate manual bottlenecks and transform how the IAM team operates. The seed of something big was planted this year.

2025 — The Workflow Revolution

🚀Built Workflows That Changed Everything

This was the year of building — lots and lots of it. Delivered game-changing Power Automate workflows: Emergency Termination flows, Rescind Emails, Handover Mail automation, and Mail Alerts from Higher Officials. Every single one cut manual effort and improved team efficiency in a real, measurable way. This is the work I'm most proud of — and it keeps running long after my account gets deprovisioned.

March 11, 2026 — Last Working Day

🌅Session Ended. Legacy Lives On.

They say all good things must come to an end — but this isn't an end, it's a new beginning. Today my account gets deprovisioned, but the workflows keep running, the access policies remain in place, and the gratitude is permanent. Thank you all. Every single one of you. It's been the honour of a career.

✦ Special Thanks

Mohd Izhar Saifi 👑

My Manager. My biggest believer.

You saw potential in me when I was still figuring things out, and you backed it with action — giving me top ratings two consecutive years in a row. That kind of confidence from a manager doesn't just motivate — it transforms. I carry that belief into everything I do next. Thank you, Izhar. Truly.

From the Heart

A Letter to My Team 💌

Nearly 5 years. One team. Thank you all.

Dear HCLTech Access & Provisioning family,

July 27, 2021 — my access was granted, my journey began as a Senior Analyst at HCLTech. Today, March 11, 2026, that account gets deprovisioned. But what happened in between? That's what I want to talk about.

In 2022, I found my footing — and my people. Escalations kept us busy, but they also introduced me to Pandia and Karthiga, who made the day-to-day genuinely enjoyable. In 2023, I got truly hands-on with the provisioning systems — understanding how every employee onboards and terminates, how access gets granted, reviewed, and revoked. That year turned theory into expertise.

In 2024, I started asking the question that changed everything: what if we could automate the parts that slow us down? I researched, tested, and mapped out what Power Automate could do for this team. And in 2025, it all came together — Emergency Termination workflows, Rescind Email flows, Handover Mail automation, and Higher Official Mail Alerts. I genuinely believe these workflows made the team more efficient. That contribution means everything to me.

A special word for Mohd Izhar Saifi — my manager, my champion. You believed in me, backed it with top ratings two consecutive years, and gave me the space to grow. That is not a small thing. I carry that forward with gratitude.

To every single person on this team — thank you all. Not for the memories, but for the work we did together, the problems we solved, and the standard we held. Access & Provisioning isn't glamorous work, but we made it matter.

My account is being deprovisioned today — but my respect for this team has no expiry date. 🔐

With all the gratitude in the world,

Sathish 🚀
